# PhotoShrink – Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

PhotoShrink respects your privacy. This document explains what data the app does and does not collect.

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect
PhotoShrink **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data**.  
Specifically, the app does **not** collect:

- Name, email, or contact information  
- Device identifiers  
- Photos or metadata  
- Location information  
- Usage analytics  
- Advertising identifiers  

## 2. Photo Access
PhotoShrink requires access to your photos **only to allow you to select images for compression and upload**.  
Your photos are processed **locally on your device** and are **never stored or transmitted by us**.

If you choose to upload compressed photos to Google Photos, the upload happens **directly between your device and Google’s servers** using your Google account. We do not have access to your credentials or files.

## 3. Google Sign-In
PhotoShrink uses Google Sign-In solely to allow you to upload images to your Google Photos account.  
The app does **not** collect, store, or share your Google account information.

All authentication is handled securely via Google OAuth.

## 4. In-App Purchases
PhotoShrink offers optional, one-time in-app purchases.  
Apple processes all payments.  
We do **not** receive or store your payment information.

## 5. Third-Party Services
The app uses:

- **Google Photos API** for uploading images  
- **Apple StoreKit** for handling in-app purchases  

These services have their own privacy policies, which you may review separately.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
PhotoShrink does not target children under 13 and collects no data from them.
